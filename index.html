Usage

Common Clock

A 24h format clock based on the traditional system, ajusted according to the browser's current time.

Centi Clock

A clock with 100 hours (centi-hours) a day, based on a base-100 system, as most of the units in the International System of Units. In comparison with the traditional system:

  • A centi-hour is equivalent to 14 minutes and 24 seconds;
  • A centi-minute is equivalent to 8.64 seconds;
  • A centi-second is quivalent to 86.4 milliseconds

Range Clock

Considering as input a start time and a finish time (both in 24-hours format), this system provides a 100 hours clock showing the range's percentage achieved at current time of the day.
